SleepLog FAQ

What can I post? You can post whatever you would like. SleepLog is a diary for your dreams!

Who can see my posts? We have two settings: public and private. You can choose to share your dream or keep it private and use SleepLog as your personal diary!

What kinds of dreams are there? There is lucid dreaming and normal dreaming. These dreams differ by if you have conciousness or not.

What are the cateories in the dream diary? For your convenience, SleepLog has categories for you to organize your dreams and to search for other dreams that could be similar to yours.

Where can I find more information about the types of dreams I am having? Please refer to our references page.
